[b][size=5]Eury's Multi-Textured Levers v1.3 (Iron, Gold and Diamond)[/size][/b]
[b][size=3]Works just like your average lever except it looks how you want it to look[/size].[/b]

[size=5][b]Please note that Multi-Textured Levers is now packaged with all other mods on the main download page above.[/b][/size]

Then the mod files to be placed in [b]%appdata%\.minecraft\mods\[/b]:
